Why the Wrong Ring Size Is a Costly Mistake

A ring that doesn’t fit properly often leads to resizing fees, shipping delays, or worse — a ring that can’t be resized at all. Many people rely on guesswork or averages, which is risky, especially for engagement rings or gifts.

Why Online Ring Size Tools Can Be Slightly Off

Online ring size calculators and printable charts are helpful, but they rely on screen calibration, printer accuracy, and perfect user technique. Even small errors of 0.5 mm can result in an uncomfortable fit.

Jewelers confirm that the most reliable method is still a physical ring sizing tool that measures your finger directly.
